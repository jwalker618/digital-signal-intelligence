## 🎯 Project Overview
### The Core Thesis
A company's digital presence—how they maintain their website, who they're connected to online, and what they disclose—serves as a powerful proxy for operational maturity, governance quality, and risk management capability. By systematically scoring these "digital signals," we can automate 75-85% of underwriting decisions while simultaneously improving risk selection.

## 🏗️ Technical Approach
### Three-Pillar Model

1/. Loss History (Traditional)
2/. Loss Prevention (Traditional + Enhanced)
3/. Exposure Characterization (Revolutionary - Digital Signals)

### Four Signal Categories
#### Infrastructure Signals
* SSL certificates, security headers, technology stack
* Predicts: Cyber exposure, operational discipline

#### Content Signals
* Governance transparency, disclosure depth, update frequency
* Predicts: D&O risk, compliance culture

#### Network Signals
* Link quality, vendor ecosystem, supply chain connections
* Predicts: Credit risk, political risk, counterparty exposure

#### Behavioral Signals
* Maintenance discipline, incident response, operational consistency
* Predicts: All lines - general management quality
