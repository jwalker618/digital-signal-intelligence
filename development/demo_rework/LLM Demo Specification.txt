SPEC_NAME: LLM Ingestion Method
SPEC_TYPE: ingestion_protocol
APPLIES_TO: all topic specs, layout specs, visual specs, interaction specs

====================================================================
INGESTION FORMAT
====================================================================

FORMAT_TYPE: plain_text
ENCODING: UTF-8
STRUCTURE: deterministic block structure
PARSING_MODEL: section ? subsection ? key:value pairs

BLOCK_DELIMITERS:
  - topic blocks separated by triple line: ===
  - section blocks separated by double line: ==
  - key:value pairs separated by newline
  - no nested indentation
  - no markdown, HTML, or rich formatting

REQUIRED BLOCKS PER TOPIC:
  - TOPIC_NAME
  - TOPIC_ID
  - TOPIC_TYPE
  - TOPIC_NAVIGATION
  - TOPIC_LAYOUT
  - TOPIC_INTERACTIONS
  - TOPIC_VISUAL
  - GLOBAL_VISUAL_BACKGROUND
  - SECTION_1 ? SECTION_N
  - INTERACTION_RULES
  - END_OF_SPEC

INTERACTION INGESTION
====================================================================

  - static_card
  - expandable_card
  - side_by_side_card
  - navigation_chevrons
  - next_section / previous_section

  - all states must be deterministic
