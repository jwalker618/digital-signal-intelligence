How Tier Assignment Works
The composite risk score (0-1000) is calculated from weighted signal groups. This score determines the initial tier assignment, which influences pricing and underwriting decisions. However, individual signal conditions can override the score-based tier - for example, a critical security vulnerability might force a decline regardless of the overall score.
