What happened?

Between April 19–21, 2025 (Easter weekend), Marks & Spencer (M&S) suffered a major ransomware attack that disrupted its IT systems, halted online orders, and impacted in-store services like contactless payments and Click & Collect. The attack forced M&S to take systems offline and rebuild core infrastructure, causing weeks of operational disruption and an estimated £270–£440 million financial impact.
