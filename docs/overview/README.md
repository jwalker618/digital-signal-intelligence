## Document Summaries

### Executive Summary

One-page overview covering:
- The DSI value proposition
- Expected business outcomes (combined ratio improvement, ROI)

### The PageRank Precedent

Explains the intellectual foundation of DSI:
- How Google revolutionized search by using network relationships
- How DSI applies similar principles to insurance underwriting
- Why observable signals are better predictors than self-reported data

### An Agentic Future White Paper

Comprehensive technical explanation:
- The seven signal types and their sources
- The 14-step pricing workflow

- Validation evidence and case studies

### Foundational Principles

Core methodology documentation:
- "What companies show us" vs "what companies tell us"
- Absence is signal principle
- Composite scoring approach
- Tier-to-decision mapping
